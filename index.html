One of the most enduring categories of vintage and antique toys encompasses toys that move. Pull toys, or wheeled toys powered by the user, have been popular since the time of the ancient Egyptians, whose tombs included miniature animals on wheels with mouths that opened and shut. The Egyptians also played with spinning tops, clay rattles, and balls made from clay, reeds, and leather. .

About Us:

Our mission is simple: to provide the best products and service to our customers at the lowest prices possible. We take great pride in our company, our commitment to customer service and in the products we sell. Our online store is designed to provide you with a safe and secure environment to browse our extensive product catalog.

Mikhael & Gorgia have been toy collectors for a long time. We started this as a business in 1953 after Gorgia bought a Grant Hill 12″ basketball figure. When friends saw it, they loved it and asked where we bought it. The light bulb turned on and we realized, “What a great business idea!!!” We started buying collectible toys on a large scale for resale. We now have over 1000 sq. feet of storage for our stock and it continues to grow!
